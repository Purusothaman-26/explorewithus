Mysore

About Mysore

Mysore, located in Karnataka, is a city rich in heritage, royal history, and vibrant traditions. Known as the Cultural Capital of Karnataka, it’s famous for the grand Mysore Palace and the colorful Dussehra festival.

With its palaces, gardens, temples, and silk sarees, Mysore offers an enriching cultural experience wrapped in regal charm.

Don't miss the mesmerizing Mysore Palace illumination, explore the historic Chamundi Hill, stroll through the peaceful Brindavan Gardens, and indulge in Mysore Pak. The city blends grandeur, spirituality, and tradition effortlessly.

Top Travel Destinations in Mysore

  • Mysore Palace – Magnificent Indo-Saracenic royal residence
  • Chamundi Hill – Sacred hill with panoramic city views and a temple
  • Brindavan Gardens – Famous for musical fountains and floral beauty
  • St. Philomena's Church – One of the tallest churches in Asia
  • Mysore Zoo – Well-maintained zoo with diverse wildlife
  • Rail Museum – Vintage trains and engines on display
  • Karanji Lake – Serene lake with boating and butterfly park
  • Lalitha Mahal – Heritage hotel with royal architecture

Travel Tips for Visiting Mysore

  • Best time to visit: October to February for pleasant weather.
  • Clothing: Light cottons for the day; a jacket for evenings in winter.
  • Transport: Auto-rickshaws and app-based taxis are easily available.
  • Local culture: Respect traditions, especially around temples.
  • Festivals: Visit during Dussehra for cultural events and processions.
  • Food: Try Mysore Masala Dosa and authentic South Indian thali.
  • Shopping: Great for silk sarees, sandalwood, and handicrafts.
  • Timing: Check palace lighting schedule for evening visits.
