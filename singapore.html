Singapore

About Singapore

Singapore is a sleek, modern island nation known for its clean streets, futuristic architecture, and multicultural society.

From the iconic Marina Bay Sands to lush gardens and bustling hawker centers, it’s a fusion of technology and tradition.

Discover Gardens by the Bay, ride the Singapore Flyer, stroll through Little India and Chinatown, or enjoy shopping on Orchard Road. Singapore offers something for every kind of traveler in a compact, efficient setting.

Top Travel Destinations in Singapore

  • Marina Bay Sands – Iconic hotel, SkyPark, and infinity pool views
  • Gardens by the Bay – Supertree Grove and Cloud Forest
  • Sentosa Island – Beaches, Universal Studios, and S.E.A. Aquarium
  • Singapore Zoo & Night Safari – World-renowned wildlife experiences
  • Orchard Road – High-end shopping and dining
  • Little India & Chinatown – Cultural districts with temples and markets
  • Clarke Quay – Riverside dining and nightlife
  • Merlion Park – Iconic photo spot at Marina Bay

Travel Tips for Visiting Singapore

  • Best time to visit: February to April for pleasant weather
  • Visa: E-visa available for Indian citizens
  • Transport: MRT trains, buses, and ride-hailing apps
  • Currency: Singapore Dollar (SGD)
  • Language: English, Mandarin, Malay, and Tamil
  • Rules: Follow public laws (e.g., no littering or chewing gum)
  • Food: Try Hainanese Chicken Rice, Laksa, and Satay
  • Technology: Download the SG Arrival Card before travel
