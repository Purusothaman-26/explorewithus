Malaysia

About Malaysia

Malaysia is a vibrant Southeast Asian country known for its cultural diversity, lush rainforests, and modern cities.

From the skyscrapers of Kuala Lumpur to the beaches of Langkawi and the heritage streets of George Town, it offers a rich travel experience.

Explore the iconic Petronas Towers, indulge in delicious Malay, Chinese, and Indian cuisine, visit the ancient caves of Batu, or relax on serene islands. Malaysia offers the perfect blend of nature, culture, and city life.

Top Travel Destinations in Malaysia

  • Kuala Lumpur – Petronas Towers, shopping, and street food
  • Langkawi – Beaches, cable car rides, and duty-free shopping
  • Penang – George Town’s heritage and famous food culture
  • Malacca – Colonial architecture and historical charm
  • Cameron Highlands – Tea plantations and cool weather
  • Borneo (Sabah/Sarawak) – Rainforests, wildlife, and Mount Kinabalu
  • Batu Caves – Hindu shrine inside a limestone hill
  • Tioman Island – Snorkeling and scuba diving paradise

Travel Tips for Visiting Malaysia

  • Best time to visit: March to early October (dry season)
  • Visa: E-visa or visa-free entry for Indian citizens (up to 30 days)
  • Transport: Metro, buses, ride-hailing apps, and domestic flights
  • Currency: Malaysian Ringgit (MYR)
  • Language: Malay (English widely spoken)
  • Culture: Respect local customs, especially at religious sites
  • Food: Try Nasi Lemak, Satay, and Char Kway Teow
  • Shopping: Visit Bukit Bintang and Central Market in KL
