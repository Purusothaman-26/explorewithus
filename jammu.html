Jammu & Kashmir

About Jammu & Kashmir

Known as “Paradise on Earth”, Jammu & Kashmir is blessed with breathtaking landscapes, snow-capped mountains, lakes, and gardens. It’s a perfect destination for nature lovers, honeymooners, and adventure seekers.

Famous places include Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Dal Lake, and Vaishno Devi Temple — offering both spiritual and scenic experiences.

Enjoy shikara rides on Dal Lake, skiing in Gulmarg, trekking in Sonamarg, or exploring Mughal gardens. Jammu & Kashmir offers a mix of culture, beauty, and peace — making it one of India’s top travel destinations.

Top Travel Destinations in Jammu & Kashmir

  • Srinagar – Famous for Dal Lake and houseboats
  • Gulmarg – Known for skiing and snow activities
  • Pahalgam – A serene valley with rivers and greenery
  • Sonamarg – Ideal for trekking and mountain views
  • Vaishno Devi – Sacred pilgrimage site in Jammu
  • Leh & Ladakh – Breathtaking landscapes and monasteries
  • Betaab Valley – Scenic valley named after Bollywood movie
  • Yusmarg – Peaceful meadows and pine forests

Travel Tips for Visiting Jammu & Kashmir

  • Carry valid ID proof: You may need it at checkpoints and for hotel check-ins.
  • Weather-ready clothing: Pack warm clothes even in summer, especially for Gulmarg and Sonamarg.
  • Book in advance: Hotels and houseboats can fill up quickly during peak season (April–October).
  • Stay connected: Prepaid SIM cards from outside Jammu & Kashmir may not work. Buy a local SIM if needed.
  • Respect local customs: Be polite, especially in religious or culturally sensitive areas.
  • Keep cash handy: Some remote areas may not accept digital payments or cards.
  • Medical kit: Carry basic medicines and altitude sickness remedies if going to Leh or higher areas.
  • Check travel advisories: Weather and road conditions can change quickly—stay updated.
