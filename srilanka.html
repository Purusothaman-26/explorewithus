Sri Lanka

About Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is an island nation in South Asia known for its ancient temples, scenic beaches, and lush hill country.

From cultural heritage in Kandy to wildlife in Yala and beaches in Mirissa, Sri Lanka offers a rich and diverse travel experience.

Visit the ancient rock fortress of Sigiriya, explore the tea plantations in Nuwara Eliya, relax on the southern coast, or go on a safari in Yala National Park. Sri Lanka is a perfect mix of nature, culture, and adventure.

Top Travel Destinations in Sri Lanka

  • Colombo – Capital city with modern vibes and colonial charm
  • Kandy – Home to the sacred Temple of the Tooth
  • Sigiriya – Ancient rock fortress with breathtaking views
  • Nuwara Eliya – Cool-climate town with tea gardens
  • Ella – Hill station known for hiking and waterfalls
  • Galle – Dutch colonial fort city with coastal beauty
  • Yala National Park – Wildlife safaris and leopard sightings
  • Mirissa – Laid-back beach town ideal for whale watching

Travel Tips for Visiting Sri Lanka

  • Best time to visit: December to April (South & West), May to September (East coast)
  • Visa: Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) required for most visitors
  • Transport: Scenic train rides are a must; tuk-tuks are great for short distances
  • Local etiquette: Dress modestly when visiting temples
  • Currency: Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR)
  • Food: Try local dishes like rice and curry, hoppers, and kottu roti
  • Health: Stay hydrated and use mosquito repellent in rural areas
  • Connectivity: SIM cards with data are affordable and easily available
