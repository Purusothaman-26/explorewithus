Bangkok

About Bangkok

Bangkok, the capital of Thailand, is famous for its ornate temples, bustling street life, and vibrant markets.

From the Grand Palace to floating markets and nightlife, Bangkok offers a unique mix of tradition and energy.

Explore the spiritual beauty of Wat Pho, cruise the Chao Phraya River, indulge in Thai street food, and shop at mega malls or night bazaars. Bangkok is a dynamic gateway to Thailand's rich culture.

Top Travel Destinations in Bangkok

  • Grand Palace – Royal residence and iconic Thai architecture
  • Wat Arun – Temple of Dawn on the riverside
  • Wat Pho – Home of the Reclining Buddha and Thai massage
  • Chatuchak Market – One of the largest weekend markets in the world
  • Khao San Road – Backpacker hub with nightlife and street eats
  • Asiatique – Riverfront shopping and dining experience
  • Jim Thompson House – Traditional Thai house and museum
  • Chao Phraya River Cruise – Scenic evening boat ride

Travel Tips for Visiting Bangkok

  • Best time to visit: November to February (cool and dry season)
  • Visa: eVisa or visa on arrival for Indian citizens
  • Transport: BTS Skytrain, MRT metro, tuk-tuks, and river ferries
  • Currency: Thai Baht (THB)
  • Language: Thai (English is widely understood in tourist areas)
  • Etiquette: Dress modestly when visiting temples, respect local customs
  • Food: Don’t miss pad thai, mango sticky rice, and spicy curries
  • Shopping: Bargain in markets and explore both malls and local stalls
