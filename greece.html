Greece

About Greece

Greece is a Mediterranean paradise known for its ancient ruins, blue-domed churches, and crystal-clear waters.

From Athens’ historical sites to the breathtaking islands of Santorini and Mykonos, Greece is rich in culture and beauty.

Walk through history at the Acropolis, enjoy sunset views in Oia, sail the Aegean Sea, or relax on golden beaches. Greece offers a unique blend of myth, tradition, and natural wonder.

Top Travel Destinations in Greece

  • Athens – Explore the Acropolis and Parthenon
  • Santorini – Iconic white buildings with blue domes and sunset views
  • Mykonos – Famous for beaches and vibrant nightlife
  • Crete – Ancient Minoan ruins and Mediterranean beauty
  • Delphi – Historic oracle site surrounded by mountains
  • Rhodes – Medieval architecture and beach resorts
  • Meteora – Monasteries perched on towering rock formations
  • Nafplio – Seaside town with charming old streets and fortresses

Travel Tips for Visiting Greece

  • Best time to visit: April to October (avoid peak July-August for crowds)
  • Visa: Schengen visa required for most non-EU travelers
  • Transport: Use ferries to explore islands; buses and taxis in cities
  • Currency: Euro (€)
  • Language: Greek (English widely spoken in tourist areas)
  • Food: Don’t miss souvlaki, moussaka, and fresh Greek salads
  • Culture: Dress modestly when visiting monasteries or churches
  • Safety: Very safe with welcoming locals
