About Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is renowned for its romantic charm, historic architecture, and world-class cuisine.

From the Eiffel Tower to the Louvre Museum, Paris blends elegance, art, and history into an unforgettable experience.

Stroll along the Seine River, enjoy a croissant in a Parisian café, marvel at Notre-Dame Cathedral, or shop on the Champs-Élysées. Paris offers a perfect mix of culture, beauty, and sophistication that enchants every traveler.

Top Travel Destinations in Paris

  • Eiffel Tower – The iconic symbol of Paris with panoramic city views
  • Louvre Museum – Home to the Mona Lisa and countless art treasures
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture
  • Sacré-Cœur Basilica – Stunning views from Montmartre hill
  • Champs-Élysées – Famous avenue for shopping and dining
  • Seine River Cruise – Romantic boat rides through the heart of the city
  • Palace of Versailles – A magnificent royal residence just outside Paris
  • Latin Quarter – Historic streets filled with culture, cafés, and bookstores

Travel Tips for Visiting Paris

  • Best time to visit: April to June and September to November for pleasant weather.
  • Visa: Schengen visa required for Indian citizens and others outside the EU.
  • Language: French is the official language; basic phrases help.
  • Transport: Use metro and buses for easy access across the city.
  • Tickets: Book popular attractions online in advance to skip lines.
  • Safety: Beware of pickpockets, especially in crowded areas.
  • Currency: Euro (€); cards are widely accepted.
  • Local etiquette: Greet with “Bonjour” and be polite in shops and cafes.
