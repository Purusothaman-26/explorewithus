Maldives

About Maldives

The Maldives is a tropical paradise in the Indian Ocean, known for its turquoise lagoons, white-sand beaches, and luxurious overwater villas.

A haven for honeymooners and divers, this island nation offers serene escapes, vibrant coral reefs, and world-class hospitality.

Explore the pristine beaches of Maafushi, relax in luxury on private islands, dive into the Baa Atoll's coral gardens, or enjoy a candlelit dinner over crystal-clear waters. The Maldives offers a mix of adventure, romance, and tranquility.

Top Travel Destinations in Maldives

  • Male – Capital city with cultural landmarks and markets
  • Maafushi – Budget-friendly island with water sports
  • Hulhumale – Beautiful beaches and diving spots near the airport
  • Baa Atoll – UNESCO biosphere reserve with marine biodiversity
  • Addu Atoll – Historical sites and scenic resorts
  • Vaavu Atoll – Known for night snorkeling and diving channels
  • Sun Island – Ideal for couples seeking luxury and privacy
  • Ari Atoll – Famous for whale shark sightings and diving

Travel Tips for Visiting Maldives

  • Best time to visit: November to April for dry, sunny weather.
  • Visa: 30-day free visa on arrival for most nationalities.
  • Flight access: International flights arrive at Velana International Airport, Male.
  • Local laws: Alcohol and bikinis are restricted on non-resort islands.
  • Water safety: Avoid coral damage while snorkeling or diving.
  • Currency: Maldivian Rufiyaa, but USD is widely accepted.
  • Transportation: Use speedboats or seaplanes to reach islands.
  • Internet: Wi-Fi is available in most resorts and cafes.
