Italy

About Italy

Italy is a European gem known for its historic cities, Renaissance art, and world-famous cuisine.

From the canals of Venice to the ruins of Rome and the beauty of the Amalfi Coast, Italy is a traveler’s dream.

Explore the art treasures of Florence, toss a coin in Rome’s Trevi Fountain, enjoy a gondola ride in Venice, or indulge in authentic Italian pizza and pasta. Italy blends rich history, vibrant culture, and scenic landscapes.

Top Travel Destinations in Italy

  • Rome – Home to the Colosseum, Vatican City, and ancient ruins
  • Venice – Iconic canals, gondolas, and St. Mark's Basilica
  • Florence – Renaissance art, museums, and architecture
  • Milan – Fashion capital and vibrant urban culture
  • Amalfi Coast – Stunning seaside towns with breathtaking views
  • Pisa – Famous for the Leaning Tower
  • Naples – Gateway to Pompeii and Italian culinary delights
  • Lake Como – Peaceful lakeside escape surrounded by mountains

Travel Tips for Visiting Italy

  • Best time to visit: April to June and September to October
  • Visa: Schengen visa required for most non-EU travelers
  • Transport: Use trains for city-to-city travel; walk or use metro in cities
  • Currency: Euro (€)
  • Language: Italian (basic English spoken in tourist areas)
  • Food: Try authentic pasta, pizza, and gelato
  • Safety: Watch for pickpockets in crowded tourist areas
  • Culture: Dress modestly for churches; respect local customs
