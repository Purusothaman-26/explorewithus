Switzerland

About Switzerland

Switzerland is a picturesque country known for its snow-capped Alps, serene lakes, and world-famous chocolates.

It blends natural beauty with charming cities like Zurich, Lucerne, and Geneva, offering a peaceful and luxurious travel experience.

Ride the scenic Glacier Express train, explore the alpine villages of Zermatt and Interlaken, enjoy Swiss fondue, and take a boat trip on Lake Geneva. Switzerland is a dream destination for nature lovers and honeymooners alike.

Top Travel Destinations in Switzerland

  • Zermatt – Matterhorn views and skiing adventures
  • Lucerne – Chapel Bridge, Lake Lucerne, and Mount Pilatus
  • Interlaken – Adventure sports and lakeside beauty
  • Zurich – Cultural capital with museums and vibrant nightlife
  • Geneva – UN headquarters and lakeside luxury
  • Bern – UNESCO-listed medieval old town
  • Montreux – Jazz Festival and Lake Geneva cruises
  • Jungfraujoch – “Top of Europe” train ride and snowy peaks

Travel Tips for Visiting Switzerland

  • Best time to visit: April to October for hiking, December to March for skiing
  • Visa: Schengen visa required for Indian citizens
  • Transport: Trains (Swiss Rail), scenic cable cars, and boats
  • Currency: Swiss Franc (CHF)
  • Language: German, French, Italian, and Romansh (English widely spoken)
  • Weather: Carry layers, even in summer, due to alpine climates
  • Food: Try Swiss cheese, chocolates, and Rösti
  • Passes: Consider a Swiss Travel Pass for unlimited train and transport access
