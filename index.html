Popular Destinations

VARKALA

**Varkala** is a serene coastal town in Kerala, India, known for its stunning **cliffside beaches**, natural springs, and spiritual vibes. It's a peaceful blend of **nature, wellness, and culture**—perfect for relaxing and recharging.

GOA

**Alleppey**, often called the **"Venice of the East,"** is famous for its tranquil **backwaters, houseboat cruises**, and scenic **paddy fields**. It offers a unique experience of Kerala’s rural charm and natural beauty.

LADAKH

**Wayanad** is a lush hill district in Kerala, known for its **misty mountains, waterfalls, wildlife sanctuaries**, and **spice plantations**. It's a perfect escape for nature lovers and adventure seekers.
