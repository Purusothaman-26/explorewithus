About Japan

Japan is an island nation where ancient traditions meet modern innovation, offering a unique cultural experience.

From serene temples and cherry blossoms to futuristic cities like Tokyo, Japan fascinates every traveler.

Discover the charm of Kyoto’s historic streets, experience bullet train rides, witness Mount Fuji’s beauty, or indulge in world-renowned sushi and ramen. Japan promises unforgettable adventures in every season.

Top Travel Destinations in Japan

  • Tokyo – Skytree, Shibuya Crossing, and Anime culture
  • Kyoto – Temples, geisha districts, and tea ceremonies
  • Osaka – Street food, Universal Studios, and vibrant nightlife
  • Mount Fuji – Scenic hiking and iconic views
  • Nara – Friendly deer and ancient temples
  • Hokkaido – Ski resorts and natural beauty
  • Hiroshima – Peace Memorial Park and history
  • Okinawa – Tropical beaches and marine life

Travel Tips for Visiting Japan

  • Best time to visit: March–May (cherry blossoms) or October–November (autumn colors)
  • Visa: Required for Indian citizens; apply in advance
  • Transport: JR Pass for trains, metros, buses, and taxis
  • Currency: Japanese Yen (¥)
  • Language: Japanese (basic English spoken in tourist areas)
  • Etiquette: Bow politely, avoid loud phone use in public transport
  • Food: Try sushi, ramen, tempura, and matcha treats
  • Connectivity: Consider renting a pocket Wi-Fi for navigation
