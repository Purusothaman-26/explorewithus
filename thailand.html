About Thailand

Thailand is a Southeast Asian gem known for its tropical beaches, ornate temples, and delicious street food.

From bustling Bangkok to serene islands like Phuket and Krabi, Thailand offers an unforgettable mix of culture and adventure.

Explore golden temples in Chiang Mai, enjoy nightlife on Khao San Road, snorkel in clear waters, or experience the charm of floating markets. Thailand blends tradition, natural beauty, and modern energy.

Top Travel Destinations in Thailand

  • Bangkok – Grand Palace, Wat Arun, street food, and nightlife
  • Phuket – Beautiful beaches and island hopping tours
  • Chiang Mai – Temples, night markets, and elephant sanctuaries
  • Krabi – Limestone cliffs and emerald waters
  • Pattaya – Beachside entertainment and water activities
  • Ayutthaya – Ancient capital with historical ruins
  • Koh Samui – Luxury resorts and tranquil beaches
  • Phi Phi Islands – Famous snorkeling and scenic beauty

Travel Tips for Visiting Thailand

  • Best time to visit: November to February (cool and dry season)
  • Visa: Visa-on-arrival available for Indian citizens
  • Transport: Tuk-tuks, metro, boats, and domestic flights
  • Currency: Thai Baht (THB)
  • Language: Thai (English spoken in tourist areas)
  • Culture: Dress modestly when visiting temples
  • Food: Try Pad Thai, green curry, and mango sticky rice
  • Shopping: Don’t miss Chatuchak Market in Bangkok
