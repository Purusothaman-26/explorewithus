Dubai

About Dubai

Dubai, a futuristic city in the UAE, is known for its luxury shopping, ultramodern architecture, and vibrant nightlife.

From towering skyscrapers like Burj Khalifa to desert safaris and man-made islands, Dubai offers an extraordinary mix of innovation and tradition.

Explore iconic landmarks like the Burj Al Arab and Dubai Marina, enjoy dune bashing in the desert, shop in the world’s largest malls, or experience the cultural charm of Al Fahidi district. Dubai blends opulence with Arabian heritage.

Top Travel Destinations in Dubai

  • Burj Khalifa – Tallest building in the world with observation decks
  • Dubai Mall – Massive shopping and entertainment complex
  • Palm Jumeirah – Iconic man-made island with resorts and beaches
  • Desert Safari – Dune bashing, camel rides, and Bedouin camp experiences
  • Dubai Marina – Waterfront dining and luxurious cruises
  • Global Village – Multicultural shopping, dining, and performances
  • Dubai Frame – Architectural landmark offering skyline views
  • Al Fahidi Historical District – A glimpse into traditional Emirati life

Travel Tips for Visiting Dubai

  • Best time to visit: November to March (pleasant weather)
  • Visa: E-visa available for Indian passport holders
  • Transport: Use metro, taxis, or rent cars for convenience
  • Currency: UAE Dirham (AED)
  • Dress code: Modest clothing in public; swimwear for beaches/pools
  • Culture: Respect local laws and Islamic customs
  • Shopping: Great deals during Dubai Shopping Festival (Jan–Feb)
  • Safety: Extremely safe and tourist-friendly
