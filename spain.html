About Spain

Spain is a vibrant European country known for its rich history, flamenco music, and breathtaking architecture.

From the beaches of Costa del Sol to Gaudí’s creations in Barcelona, Spain offers cultural flair and endless exploration.

Wander through the Alhambra in Granada, experience a traditional tapas night in Madrid, dance in Seville, or enjoy a football match in Barcelona. Spain blends art, heritage, and warm hospitality.

Top Travel Destinations in Spain

  • Barcelona – Sagrada Família, Park Güell, and vibrant nightlife
  • Madrid – Royal Palace, Prado Museum, and tapas culture
  • Seville – Flamenco, Alcázar Palace, and Gothic cathedral
  • Granada – Alhambra Palace and Moorish charm
  • Valencia – City of Arts and Sciences and beachside vibe
  • Bilbao – Guggenheim Museum and Basque cuisine
  • Toledo – Medieval architecture and cultural fusion
  • Ibiza – World-renowned beaches and party scene

Travel Tips for Visiting Spain

  • Best time to visit: April to June and September to November
  • Visa: Schengen visa required for Indian citizens
  • Transport: High-speed trains (AVE), metro, and local buses
  • Currency: Euro (€)
  • Language: Spanish (English spoken in tourist areas)
  • Weather: Warm summers, mild winters – pack light and breathable clothes
  • Food: Must-try dishes include paella, churros, and tortilla española
  • Festivals: Don’t miss La Tomatina and the Running of the Bulls
